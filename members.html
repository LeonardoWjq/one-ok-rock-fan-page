Meet the Band

Current Members

Taka (Takahiro Moriuchi)

Lead Vocals

Taka has been the vocalist since the band's formation in 2005. Known for his powerful and versatile voice, he can effortlessly switch between melodic singing and intense screams. His emotional delivery and stage presence have made him one of the most recognizable vocalists in the Japanese rock scene.

Toru (Toru Yamashita)

Guitarist and Leader

Toru is a founding member from 2005 and serves as the band's leader. His guitar skills and songwriting abilities have been instrumental in shaping ONE OK ROCK's distinctive sound. As the band's leader, he has guided their evolution from a local Japanese act to an internationally recognized rock band.

Ryota (Kohama Ryota)

Bassist

Ryota has been with the band since 2005. His energetic bass lines provide the foundation for ONE OK ROCK's dynamic sound. Known for his enthusiastic stage presence and technical skill, Ryota brings both musical depth and visual energy to the band's performances.

Tomoya (Kanki Tomoya)

Drummer

Tomoya joined the band in 2006. His powerful and precise drumming has become a cornerstone of ONE OK ROCK's sound. With his technical proficiency and ability to drive the band's rhythm section, Tomoya has established himself as one of the most respected drummers in the Japanese rock scene.
