About the Band

The journey of ONE OK ROCK

Formed in Tokyo in 2005, ONE OK ROCK is a Japanese rock band known for their blend of alternative rock, emo, and post-hardcore. The current members are Taka (vocals), Toru (guitar), Ryota (bass), and Tomoya (drums).

Starting as a five-piece band, they've evolved into one of Japan's most internationally recognized rock acts. Their name comes from "one o'clock," the time they used to practice, but is deliberately spelled as "ONE OK ROCK" to represent their unique identity.

The band is known for their energetic live performances and Taka's ability to seamlessly switch between Japanese and English lyrics, helping them build a global fanbase beyond Japan's borders.

Latest Album: Luxury Disease (2022)

Released on September 9, 2022, "Luxury Disease" marks ONE OK ROCK's return to their rock roots while incorporating new elements into their sound. The album features hit singles like "Save Yourself" and "Wonder".

The album showcases the band's maturity and musical growth, receiving critical acclaim for its powerful compositions and Taka's vocal performance.
